Our Story

A taste of home, for everyone in the neighborhood

Habibi is a Bengali and South Asian grocery at heart, stocked with the staples and specialty ingredients our community grew up with — alongside a wide selection of Middle Eastern goods reflecting the neighbors who shop here. Everything we sell and serve is 100% Halal. Our deli and kitchen turn out fresh hot food and snacks every day, so you can grab dinner on the way home or stock up your pantry in the same trip.

From the Deli

Hot Food & Snacks

A few of the fresh favorites made in-house daily. Full grocery, deli, and specialty items available in-store.

Fuchka

Crispy hollow puris filled with spiced potato, chickpea, and tangy tamarind water.

Mughlai Paratha

Flaky stuffed flatbread filled with spiced egg and minced meat, pan-fried to order.

Samosas

Golden fried pastry packed with spiced potatoes, peas, and warming spices.

Chicken Rolls

Spiced shredded chicken rolled in a crisp pastry shell — a deli favorite.

Daal Puri

Soft fried bread stuffed with spiced lentil filling, best enjoyed warm.

Shingara

Bengali-style pastry filled with spiced potatoes and vegetables, fried until golden.
